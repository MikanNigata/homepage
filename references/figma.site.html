Why attend

What you’ll learn

At Config, you’ll hear from the world’s leading product builders on the future of design and product development. You’ll also be the first to dive into Figma’s newest products and features.

What to expect

Attend inspiring keynotes from visionaries shaping products you know and love, level up your Figma skills with hands-on demos, and dig into insightful product design topics during deep-dive sessions.

Who you’ll meet

Over 10,000 designers, developers, marketers, and product leaders attend Config every year to share creative ideas, network, and find new ways to push the boundaries of product building.
